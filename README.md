# Vibe Smart Remote 📺

A modern, high-performance Android remote control for Samsung Smart TVs, built with Jetpack Compose and Material 3.

> [!WARNING]
> **This app was completely vibe coded.** Use at your own risk and expect maximum vibes.

## ✨ Features
- **Automatic Discovery**: Automatically finds Samsung TVs on your local network.
- **Full Navigation**: DPAD control for easy menu navigation.
- **Quick Apps**: One-tap shortcuts for YouTube, Netflix, Disney+, and more.
- **Smart Keyboard**: Type directly into your TV's search fields from your phone.
- **Haptic Feedback**: Premium feel with integrated haptic responses.
- **Glassmorphic UI**: Sleek, modern design with custom gradients and glow effects.

## 🚀 Getting Started
1. Ensure your Android device and Samsung TV are on the **same Wi-Fi network**.
2. Open the app and wait for the **Discovery Screen** to find your TV.
3. Select your TV and look for a prompt on the TV screen to **"Allow"** the connection.
4. Enjoy controlling your TV with pure vibes!

## 🛠 Tech Stack
- **Language**: Kotlin
- **UI Framework**: Jetpack Compose
- **Networking**: OkHttp (WebSockets)
- **JSON Parsing**: Gson
- **Haptics**: Compose HapticFeedback API
